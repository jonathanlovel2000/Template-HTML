Tan Jianci akan menggantikan Yang Yang sebagai pemeran utama pria

Tan Jianci, who rose to fame after starring alongside Yang Zi in "Lost You Forever," is about to begin filming his new drama "Why Not Cross the River Together," co-starring with Lu Yuxiao. The original male lead was Yang Yang, but he withdrew due to concerns about the numerous action scenes and the potential impact on his old injuries. Tan Jianci, known for his good acting skills, has taken over the role, making it highly anticipated. Lu Yuxiao previously gained fame for her role as a female spy in "The Cloud of Feathers," so the freshness of their pairing makes the drama even more exciting.

Tan Jianci first gained attention for his role in the suspense drama "Hunter's Guide to Crime," and later starred alongside Yang Zi in the hit drama "Lost You Forever," which propelled him to stardom. Tan Jianci is a highly skilled actor with numerous acting opportunities. He joined the cast of the film "Empty Gun" last November, and after wrapping up filming at the end of the year, he flew directly to Hengdian for costume fittings for "Why Not Cross the River Together," earning praise from many fans for his strong work ethic and making him a role model in the entertainment industry.

Rumors of a TV adaptation of "Why Not Cross the River Together" first surfaced at the end of last year. Initially, Yang Yang was rumored to be paired with Zhang Miaoyi, but Yang Yang declined the role due to his commitment to filming the sequel to "A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality," as the male lead, Xie Queshan, is an undercover general with many action scenes. Now, Tan Jianci has taken over the role. He previously wore military uniform in "Lost You Forever," where he was praised for his dashing and heroic appearance. His portrayal of Xie Queshan is considered a perfect fit and is highly anticipated.

Furthermore, Lu Yuxiao recently rose to fame with "Entering the Clouds," and this marks her first collaboration with Tan Jianci, a very novel pairing. However, Lu Yuxiao's penguin-like gait has been a point of criticism, leading to rumors that the production team hopes Tan Jianci can help correct her posture. Lu Yuxiao previously played a spy in her representative work, "Cloud Feather," so this pairing with Tan Jianci is seen as a potential hit drama.
